About Lina Nkosi Foundation

At Lina Nkosi Foundation, our mission is simple:
to make a lasting difference in the lives of those who need it most. As a non-profit organisation, we are dedicated to providing support, guidance, and essential services to individuals and communities that face daily challenges.

Founded on the principle of giving back, our work is deeply rooted in the dream of our founder,
Nkosi, who believes in the power of compassion and action. The vision can be summed up in the words:
"My dream to do good, helping in education, shelter, counseling, and public interest services, depends solely on a good cause."
Through this passion, the foundation is built on the belief that together, we can create positive change and build stronger, more resilient communities.

What is the mission of the Lina Nkosi Foundation?

The mission of the Lina Nkosi Foundation is to support vulnerable communities by providing resources, education, and services that foster social and economic empowerment.

What types of initiatives does the foundation support?

The foundation focuses on initiatives related to education, healthcare, poverty alleviation, community development, and women's empowerment.

How can I get involved with the foundation?

You can get involved by volunteering, donating, or partnering with the foundation. Visit our "Get Involved" page for more details.

Where does the Lina Nkosi Foundation operate?

We primarily operate in underserved communities in South Africa, but our programs and initiatives have the potential to expand to other regions.

How do I apply for assistance from the foundation?

You can apply for assistance by visiting our "Support" page and filling out the relevant forms. Each application is carefully reviewed based on urgency and the available resources.

How are donations used?

Donations are used to fund our various programs, including educational scholarships, healthcare services, food drives, and housing projects. We ensure that all funds are allocated responsibly.

How do I stay updated on the foundation's work?

You can stay updated by subscribing to our newsletter or following us on social media. We regularly post updates about ongoing projects and upcoming events.

Can companies or organizations partner with the Lina Nkosi Foundation?

Yes, we welcome partnerships with companies, organizations, and other foundations that align with our mission and values. Reach out to us to explore partnership opportunities.
